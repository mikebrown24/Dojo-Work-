Kobe Bryant

Kobe Bryant is a former NBA superstar who has played for the Los Angeles Lakers for the entire span of his 20 year career. During this time span, he was won multiple NBA championships, multiple scoring titles, two NBA finals MVP's, and is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players to ever live. Kobe was born in Philadelphia, PA and has lived in Italy for an extended amount of time. Over the years, he has learned and mastered 7 languages in which he communicates with foreign teammates and media personnel.
